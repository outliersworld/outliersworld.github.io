Sounds of Mumbai

Get transported to Mumbai sonically

Tapan Babbar, a UI/UX Engineer based in Delhi has created a website called the Sounds of Mumbai <i>(http://soundsofmumbai.in)</i> which brings an auditory dimension to virtual tourism. Sounds of Mumbai lets you experience the sonic ambiences of 13 different key nodes of Mumbai. Visitors can sonically experience the hustle bustle of Mumbai Local or listen to the gutargoo of pigeons near The Taj.
