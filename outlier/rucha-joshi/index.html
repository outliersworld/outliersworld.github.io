Rucha Joshi

Rucha is an experience designer. She studied product design at the National Institute of Design, India. She excels in building narratives, research and information architecture. She’s interested in human centric design, system-mapping and-analysis and speculative design. She loves to debate and learn new languages, while harbouring a secret hope to be a broadway star one day.
