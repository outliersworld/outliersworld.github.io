Kalyani Tupkary

Kalyani is a designer from the National Institute of Design, India. She loves simple products that tell stories. Her classroom projects lay emphasis on the design process and involve simple changes to transform the design. She describes herself as a colour obsessed lettering aficionado with profound love for sketchbooks. Analog medium fascinates her and she is ever ready to get hands dirty.
