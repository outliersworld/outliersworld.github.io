Medhavi Gandhi

Medhavi has been working to revive the traditional arts/culture of India since 2009. While her work centres around heritage-education, developing artistic capacity building with a focus on rural enterprise development, youth entrepreneurship, cultural and arts exchanges and using folk arts for campaigning and communication.
