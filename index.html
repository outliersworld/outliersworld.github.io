Let's Invent Tomorrow.

The world is changing fast. It can no longer be contained in boxes of science, commerce and arts (think of algorithmic law, generative design or drone based spatial mapping). This new world moves at the pace of thought - if you can think it, you can make it.

 

The heroes of this world will be those who can adapt fast to the new world – who can learn how to learn.

We call these heroes - the Outliers - the creative misfits, the choreographers of the abstract realm who can make the world dance to their thoughts.

 

Traditional educational systems still value conformity. Outliers wants to offer an alternative.

 

Our goal is to build a world full of Outliers who are constantly inventing new possibilities for themselves and their communities. We are initiating this revolution by featuring Outliers, bringing their thoughts, ideas and inspiration to you.

 

We also are curating insightful articles and resources to help you get started with innovation driven creativity. Join us to be a part of the Outliers revolution. Let's invent tomorrow!
